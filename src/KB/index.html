Basic Troubleshooting

  1. Try it again
  2. Turn it off, and then back on again
  3. Check the connections
  4. Consult the guide below in the required category
  5. Google it
  6. Ask a quesiton on the Forums
  7. If it's an emergency, call the person who installed it, or last person who performed maintenance on it
